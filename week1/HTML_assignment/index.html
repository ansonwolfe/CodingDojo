One Lucky Dog's Tale

My name is Cobie. I'm also frequently known as "Little Brat" or "Monkey". As you can see from my expression, I am one happy dog...or, maybe I am just meditating under the California sun.

Do you see the backdrop? Can you tell where was this picture taken from? I am one lucky dog, don't you think? Read on for more.

Adventures Around the Bay

Life is pretty boring if it's all sleep and no play. So whenever I get the chance, I ask to go for a trip. It doesn't matter if it's the dog park or a great hike in the hills. I like fresh air. Sometimes I get jipped and only get a brisk walk around the block. I protest on those. You would too wouldn't you? I ordered steak and only got ground beef. See what I mean?

Answers to what you may be thinking

  • I am smaller than what you think and bigger than your first guess.
  • I am older than I look and younger than the sun.
  • I am only as friendly as you will let me pet you.

Some others things to note...

  1. I am not a cat!
  2. My name is NOT "Coco"!
  3. I like fruits and vegetables except for grapes and nuts which would kill me.
